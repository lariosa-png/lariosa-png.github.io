About Me

Hello my name is Alberto Lario Santos, I studied at ESAT and achieved a HND in videogames programming with a Merit mark. Through the years I worked with a lot of different programming languages but now the ones i feel more confident in using are C++ and C# and have experience with Unity and Unreal Engine

Programming videogames is my passion and I do not mind spending all the time necessary to research and create games to improve each day.

Since I was little I have always been interested in the world of videogames, and I worked hard with the help of professionals at different stages to get to the point I am today.

Apart from having an English B1 Cambridge certificate I speak with people from other parts of the world daily and my english level has improved a lot.

My favorite type of videogames are: Horror, Chaos, Fast reflexes/Fast movement, Adventure/Exploration. I don't have a favorite videogame of all time, I like a lot of games but I think every game is good in its own way and I do have preferences in the different genres but not a one and only videogame that is my favorite.
